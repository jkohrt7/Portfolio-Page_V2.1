About Me

I'm a recent Computer Science graduate of Furman University with a passion for creating interactive applications. Several years of Undergraduate research have given me a lot of experience with Java, the R statistical language, and working with groups from very different backgrounds. During this time, I became fascinated with the applications of web technologies and how they could help share my research with others. I'm currently enrolled in a full-stack web development bootcamp at UNC Chapel Hill so I can pursue this interest and start a career as a web-developer.

Besides web-dev, I am very passionate about 3D graphics and conservation. I'm always looking for ways to incorporate these into my work, whether that's through games (Unity 3D), digital dioramas (WebGL, THREE.js) or my research topics.
